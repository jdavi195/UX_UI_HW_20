TRAVELING IN A POST-PANDEMIC SOCIETY:

A Case Study To Define Travelers Needs

Problem Statement

While traveling for vacations has been popularized over the years, the reality of rising travel expenses make planning stressful.

Solution

Remove the stress of managing travel expenses by allowing travelers to create a trip experience based on their daily spending range.

Time Line

4 Weeks

Role

UX Co-Researcher / UX Designer

Research Plan

Our research aims to understand the core issues that may prevent people from traveling post-Pandemic?
