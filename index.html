TRAVELING IN A POST-PANDEMIC SOCIETY:

A Case Study To Define Travelers Needs

Problem Statement

While traveling for vacations has been popularized over the years, the reality of rising travel expenses has made budget planning stressful.

Solution

Remove the stress of managing travel expenses by allowing travelers to create a trip experience based on their daily spending range.

Time Line

4 Weeks (2 person Research Team)

Role

UX Co-Researcher / UX Designer

Research Plan

Our research aims to understand the core issues that may prevent people from traveling in a post-Pandemic society.

User Survey - Age Demographics

The average age of our user survey participants is between 25-34 making up 45.2% of the users we surveyed. The 33-44 rage followed closely making up 33.3% of surveyors.

Total Number of People Surveyed

42 total participants

Survey Tools Used

Google Forums

TIME SPENT TRIP PLANNING

We have discovered that the typical user spends 30 minutes to 1 hour a week planning for travel. This helps us better identify opportunities that could expedite the planning process for our users.

Apps Used to Plan Travel

We asked users if they have used any of the following apps/ websites to plan a trip in the past.

AirBnB Google Maps Expedia

73.8% users surveyed have used Airbnb to plan a trip in the past.

71.4% of users surveyed have used Google Maps in the past when planning a trip.

54.8% of users surveyed said they have used Expedia when planning a trip.
